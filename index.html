Hollow Knight is a 2D metroid-vania game set in the land of Hallownest, an ancient kingdom inhabited by bugs. The player controls a nameless knight and explores the kingdom fighting off enemies and uncovering the history of the kingdom along the way.

Hollow Knight's gameplay is difficult but rewarding. Exploration always rewards the player in some way either through upgrades or revealing the story of the game. The combat is easy and simple to understand but is still challenging with many hard boss fights.

The game was developed by Team Cherry. A small group of three indie indie developers made the game with help from kickstartrer. Ari Gibson, William Pellen, and Jack Vine created the gameplay with help from Christopher Larken to compose the music.
